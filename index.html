About

Zakir Jamal is a writer and editor living in Montreal. His fiction has appeared in Pastiche and ahoy and his nonfiction in the Los Angeles Post. He also edits fiction at Scrivener Creative Review. His story "Reflections on the Occasion" was named to the shortlist of the 2026 Leopold Bloom Prize for Innovative Narration by L'Esprit Literary Review, and he is the recipient of a Quebec Writer's Federation Fiction Membership for 2026.

Zakir holds a bachelor's degree in Philosophy from the University of Chicago, and expects to complete his MA in English at McGill University in the summer of 2026. He has worked in education, journalism, and copywriting. Some of the results are linked below.
